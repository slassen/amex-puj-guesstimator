Weights & decay

How to read it. The model targets profitability, not credit risk: throughput and tenure help; recent bonus-grab closures and paid retention offers hurt; old events fade on the half-life. The probability is most trustworthy near the middle and overconfident at the tails — treat a 90 as "very likely fine," not a guarantee. With the supplied defaults it lands near the high-50s, a touch above the ~50% we discussed because the generalized closure term is granular and the retention event now decays rather than counting flat.
